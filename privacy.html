Core Principle

Your conversations stay private. VibeAI FoldSpace processes everything locally on your device. We never send, store, or access your data on any server — because we don't operate any servers that receive user data.

This is not a legal caveat or a future commitment. It is the architectural foundation of the product. The extension has no backend. No API calls carrying user content. No telemetry pipeline. Every analysis that VibeAI FoldSpace performs happens inside your browser, on your machine, under your control.

2

What VibeAI FoldSpace Does

VibeAI FoldSpace helps you stay mentally engaged while using AI — it detects thinking patterns in real time (active, passive, or exploratory) and offers optional coaching nudges, focus controls, and reflection prompts. All analysis happens locally in your browser.

The extension observes conversation structure on supported AI chat platforms (ChatGPT, Claude.ai, and Gemini) to determine your current engagement stage. It never reads, stores, or transmits the actual content of your conversations.

What We Do NOT Do

✕No servers — we don't operate any backend infrastructure that receives user data
✕No data collection — we don't log, store, or transmit your conversations
✕No tracking — no analytics, telemetry, or usage monitoring of any kind
✕No third parties — we don't share data with anyone, because we have no user data to share

5

Your Privacy Controls

  • Consent required. VibeAI FoldSpace only activates after you explicitly grant permission. You may revoke consent at any time via the extension settings.
  • Focus Mode. Use the ⋯ menu to temporarily pause all nudges for 2 or 12 hours. Stored locally as a timer. Expires automatically.
  • Coach Intensity. Choose Off, Minimal, Standard, or Active from the ⋯ menu. Saved locally and applied immediately.
  • To disable. Remove the extension from Chrome or Edge, or use the ⋯ menu to turn off coaching entirely without uninstalling.
  • To delete all data. All data is local — uninstalling removes everything. You may also clear chrome.storage.local and localStorage manually via browser DevTools (Application tab).
6

Policy Statements

The following statements reflect the data disclosure filed with the Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add-ons Store:

  1. Local processing only. All analysis runs entirely within your browser. No conversation data is sent to any server.
  2. No data transmission. VibeAI FoldSpace does not transmit, upload, or share any user data externally.
  3. No tracking. We do not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
  4. No data selling. We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties.
  5. Local storage only. Consent preferences and session settings are stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device.
  6. No conversation content transmitted. No conversation content is transmitted externally under any circumstance.
  7. User-deletable. All stored data is user-deletable via browser settings or the extension's reset function.
  8. Device-bound. This data never leaves your device.
